About Us

VISION STATEMENT

To offer peoples of all economic backgrounds the opportunity to enjoy the amazing splendors and rich cultures of the South with sustainability in a true Mississippi fashion.

COMPANY HISTORY

Wild River Rapids began with a dream; a dream to run rivers, lakes and create opportunity for guests of all ages and abilities to reconnect with nature and experience the outdoors. This dream became a reality in the spring of 2020. The company has grown and expanded based on our guests suggestions and drive to experience more! We love everything about our lifestyle and want to share a piece of our heart with you on all of our guided experiences. All guides are certified to the highest standards including Royal Lifesaving Society Swift Water Rescue, Wilderness First Aid, and Leave No Trace. Other certifications may include Mississippi Heritage Interpretation, Service Best and Paddle Mississippi certifications. We have now expanded to offer a voyageur fur trade program complete with voyageur canoe at Bolton River and we have had a brand new mobile office open in Three Rivers Park!
